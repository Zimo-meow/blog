    📘 Vocabulary: Graffiti – Street Art or Crime¶

    wielding¶

    • 词性：v.
    • 释义：挥舞，使用（工具）
    • 例句：He was wielding a spray can confidently.

    spray¶

    • 词性：n./v.
    • 释义：喷雾；喷洒
    • 例句：She used spray paint on the wall.

    polarised¶

    • 词性：adj./v.
    • 释义：分化，对立
    • 例句：The issue has polarised public opinion.

    verdict¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：裁决，判决
    • 例句：The jury returned a verdict of not guilty.

    conspiracy¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：阴谋，共谋
    • 例句：They were arrested for conspiracy to damage property.

    mural¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：壁画
    • 例句：A large mural was painted on the building.

    popped down¶

    • 词性：phr.v.
    • 释义：顺路去
    • 例句：We popped down to the Tate during lunch.

    irony¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：讽刺
    • 例句：There’s irony in the juxtaposition of law and art.

    juxtaposition¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：并列（对比）
    • 例句：The juxtaposition of the two events is striking.

    sneaking¶

    • 词性：v.
    • 释义：偷偷进入
    • 例句：He kept sneaking his art into museums.

    retrospective¶

    • 词性：adj./n.
    • 释义：回顾的；回顾展
    • 例句：The museum held a retrospective of his work.

    legitimised¶

    • 词性：v.
    • 释义：使合法化
    • 例句：Banksy legitimised the genre of street art.

    spawned¶

    • 词性：v.
    • 释义：催生，产生
    • 例句：His work spawned a wave of imitators.

    immodesty¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：不谦虚
    • 例句：He claimed with genuine immodesty.

    draconian¶

    • 词性：adj.
    • 释义：严厉的
    • 例句：They face draconian punishments.

    convict¶

    • 词性：n./v.
    • 释义：囚犯；定罪
    • 例句：The convict was sentenced to two years.

    curator¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：策展人
    • 例句：The curator organized a street art exhibition.

    schizophrenic¶

    • 词性：adj.
    • 释义：矛盾的，分裂的
    • 例句：Society has a schizophrenic attitude to graffiti.

    estate¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：房地产
    • 例句：He works in real estate.

    dichotomy¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：二分法，对立
    • 例句：There is a dichotomy between freedom and order.

    reconciled¶

    • 词性：v./adj.
    • 释义：调和；和解
    • 例句：The two ideas were reconciled.

    loath¶

    • 词性：adj.
    • 释义：憎恶的，讨厌的
    • 例句：Citizens were loath to accept graffiti.

    deterioration¶

    • 词性：n.
    • 释义：恶化，衰退
    • 例句：They see graffiti as a sign of deterioration.
